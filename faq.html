Travel & Logistics

What is the best time to visit Melaka?

While Melaka is a year-round destination, the best time is during the dry season between April and October.

Is there a tourist tax in Melaka?

Yes, Melaka implements a Heritage Tax (Caj Warisan) of RM2 per room per night for hotels.

Food & Culture

Are most food outlets Halal?

Melaka offers a wide variety of Halal food. For Nyonya cuisine, many restaurants are Pork-Free, but it's best to check for the certificate.
